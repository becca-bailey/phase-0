Yahtzee!

Press 'play' to roll five dice. Once you have rolled, you may choose which dice to keep, and which to roll again. You have three rolls to win.

Yahtzee - All dice the same

Straight - Four or five dice that count up successively

Full House - Three of one number and two of another

Three of a Kind - Three dice the same

Four of a Kind - Four dice the same

Good luck!
